About Data Guardian

Your trusted partner for Zoho CRM data quality management

Our Story

Data Guardian was born from a simple observation: bad data kills sales pipelines. After years of working with Zoho CRM, we noticed that even the best CRM systems suffer from data quality issues — invalid emails, wrong phone numbers, incomplete addresses, and missing critical fields.

We built Data Guardian to solve this problem automatically. Instead of manually checking every record, our extension does the heavy lifting: scoring, verifying, and alerting your team when data needs attention.

🎯 Our Mission

"To make CRM data quality effortless, automatic, and actionable for every Zoho CRM user."

What We Do

Data Guardian is a Zoho CRM Sigma Extension that provides automated data quality management across Leads, Contacts, Accounts, and Deals modules. We combine health scoring, email validation, address verification, phone format checking, and business registration validation — all in one extension.

Our extension generates weekly and monthly consolidated reports so you always know the health of your CRM data at a glance.
